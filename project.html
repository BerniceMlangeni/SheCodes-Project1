Firstly let's find out what Cooking is.

Cooking, also known as cookery or professionally as the culinary arts, is the art, science and craft
of using heat to make food more palatable, digestible, nutritious, or safe. Cooking techniques
and ingredients vary widely, from grilling food over an open fire, to using electric stoves, to baking
in various types of ovens, reflecting local conditions. Cooking is an aspect of all human societies
and a cultural universal.
